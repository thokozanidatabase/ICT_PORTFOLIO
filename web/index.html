Who we are

We are a government department under the Ministry of Justice and Constitutional Affairs responsible for the registration and administration of business entities, Non Profit Organizations and Industrial Property Rights.

What we do

We are responsible for registration of businesses, non-profit organizations and managing industrial property rights. We collect non-tax revenue like stamp duty. We operate from our offices in Blantyre, Lilongwe and Mzuzu.
